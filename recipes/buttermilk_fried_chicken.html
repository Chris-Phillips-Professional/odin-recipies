Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Brine:

  • Buttermilk
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Dried oregano (or Italian seasoning)
  • Black Pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder

Batter

  • 1:3 cornstarch to flour mix
  • A couple teaspoons of baking powder
  • Add a couple of tablespoons of buttermilk to the batter
  • Double coat the chicken in buttermilk and the flour mix

Cooking:

  • Fry chicken on medium heat for 8 minutes, let rest, and then fry for an additional 8 minutes
  • If chicken isn't cooked, continue cooking in oven

Optional:

  • Place chicken in the fridge for an hour and then re-fry for 5 minutes for extra crunch
